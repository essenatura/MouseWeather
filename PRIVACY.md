Privacy Policy for MouseWeather

Last updated: June 25, 2026

MouseWeather ("the App") is an open-source weather application designed with privacy as a priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information.
1. Data Collection and Usage
MouseWeather does not collect, store, or share any personal identifiable information (PII). We do not use trackers, analytics, or advertising IDs.
2. Location Data
To provide accurate weather information, the App requires location data:
•
Manual Entry: You can manually enter a city name or coordinates.
•
GPS Location: If granted, the App uses your device's location to fetch local weather.
•
Usage: Location data is processed locally on your device and sent to third-party weather APIs (Open-Meteo and MET Norway) to retrieve weather data.
•
Storage: We do not store your location on any external servers. Your coordinates are used only for the immediate weather request.
3. Third-Party Services
The App fetches data from the following providers. Please refer to their respective privacy policies:
•
Open-Meteo: https://open-meteo.com/en/privacy
•
MET Norway: https://www.met.no/en/About-us/Privacy-policy
4. Background Processing
The App uses Android WorkManager to update weather data and notifications in the background. This process uses the last known location stored locally on your device.
5. Ads and Trackers
MouseWeather is 100% free of advertisements and third-party tracking libraries.
6. Open Source
The source code of MouseWeather is public and available for audit at: https://github.com/essenatura/MouseWeather
7. Contact
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via GitHub Issues on our repository.
